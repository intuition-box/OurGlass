---
title: Add OurGlass to your Safe
description: Install the OurGlass Safe App and open it from your Safe.
---

OurGlass runs as a [Safe App](https://app.safe.global) inside your Safe's
interface, so charges are authorized by your existing Safe signers.

## Install

1. Open your Safe at [app.safe.global](https://app.safe.global).
2. Go to **Apps** and add a custom app.
3. Use the OurGlass app URL:

```
https://ourglass.intuition.box
```

Safe reads the app manifest from that URL and loads OurGlass in a sandboxed
iframe. The same URL serves the public site in a normal browser tab and the Safe
App when loaded inside Safe.
