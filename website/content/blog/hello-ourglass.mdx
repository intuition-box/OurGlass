OurGlass is recurring on-chain payments for Safe: sign one delegation, and a
receiver can pull a bounded amount over time — capped on-chain, non-custodial,
revocable.

This site now runs on Fumadocs, so alongside the landing page you'll find proper
[documentation](/docs) and this blog, all served from one domain.

## What's documented

- [What OurGlass is](/docs) and the shared [delegation](/docs/concepts/delegation)
  model.
- The two payment shapes: [subscriptions](/docs/concepts/subscriptions)
  (per-period cap) and [streams](/docs/concepts/streams) (linear accrual).
- The [security](/docs/security) trust model — what is enforced on-chain and what
  it does not promise.
- The planned [analytics](/docs/analytics) approach for verifiable, on-chain
  metrics.

The app itself stays where it is — add it to your Safe from
`https://ourglass.intuition.box`. See [Add OurGlass to your Safe](/docs/guides/safe-app).
